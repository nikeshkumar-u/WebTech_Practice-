Chocolate Chip Cookies

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F(180°C).
  2. Mix flour and sugar in a bowl.
  3. Add chocolate chips and stir well
  4. Bake foe 10 minutes or until golden brown.

Incedients

Incredient Quantity
Flour 2 cups
Sugar 1 cup
Chocolate Chips 1 cup

Enjoy your home made cookies!
